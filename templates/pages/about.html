About this archive

The San Francisco Microscopical Society archives provide access to thousands of records from the history of the Society, from 1870 to today, as well as materials that were owned by the Society's members.

Some of the materials available may reflect outdated, biased, offensive, and possibly violent views and practices and are preserved for their historical significance.

If you discover personal, private, or harmful content that should be flagged for our further review, please email hello@sfmicrosociety.org with the subject line "History Review Request".

You can see how the site works in the sfms-history repository on GitHub.
